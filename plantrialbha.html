Bhadra Tour Packages

The lush green vegetation and lofty hill ranges are a beautiful and spectacular sight. The wildlife sanctuary is called so after the Bhadra River which flows through the lush forests. The lush green vegetation of the Bhadra Wildlife Sanctuary consists of mostly moist and dry deciduous forests. More than 120 species of trees grow here, which include Teak, Rosewood, Mathi, Honne, Nandi, Tadasalu and Kindal. Various kinds of dormitory and suite accommodation are available at different locations within Bhadra Wildlife sanctuary, all are owned by the state Forest Department.

Safari Timings:
Morning 6.00 a.m to 8.00 a.m
Evening 3.00 p.m to 5.00 p.m for one hour Ride.


Safari Charges:
Indian Nationals - Rs. 300/- per person
Foreign Nationals Rs.1100/- per person

Safari timings and charges are indicative and may change any time, they may cancel in last minutes due to bad weather conditions, heavy rains, non-availability of vehicles, manpower, etc., hence request you to contact Karnataka Forest Department and plan your day.
