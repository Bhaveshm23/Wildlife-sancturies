How to Reach Periyar National Park



Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary (also known as the Thekkady Wildlife Sanctuary) is one of the premium wildlife sanctuaries in India and perhaps the finest in south India.


Situated in the picturesque environs of the Western Ghats hills in Kerala, the Periyar national park covers an area of approximately 777 sq km

The varied physical features, such as landscape and climate, of this jungle hold up a rich and varied flora and fauna. The topography of the park is highly undulating and boasts numerous narrow water streams.


By Road:

Kumily, the nearest town from Periyar is well served by both state and private buses from Kottayam, Ernakulam and Madurai in Tamil Nadu.


By Rail:

Kottayam at 114-kms is the nearest railhead from Periyar.


By Air:

Kochi (Cochin) at 200-kms or Madurai in Tamil Nadu at140-kms are nearest airports from Periyar.
