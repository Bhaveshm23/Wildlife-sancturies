Jim Corbett Tour Packages

Welcome to tiger territory, Corbett is where you should visit to observe wildlife in its natural habitat. Unlock the doors of diverse wildlife in Corbett National Park and scout the exquisiteness of the natural empire. The wildlife tour to Corbett is amazing and wonderful, offering you an opportunity to take a subtle glimpse of various sorts of species. Corbett is a favored wildlife destination among wildlife enthusiasts and nature lovers for a long time. The perfect topography with a river and its tributaries winds through its entire length, Corbett promises an adventure that you will never forget. Jim Corbett Tour Packages offer a plethora of options; just pick a desired holiday package from our extensive database. If you are dreaming of a cheap vacation in paradise, you may opt for government run property within the national park or you may get real budget trips with us. We are specializes in organizing jungle safari into the national park that is conducted by park authority every morning and afternoon, accompanied by professional guide. As per your choice, you can opt for a jeep or an elephant safari as these two are the only feasible options to observe the big elusive cat in the dense jungle. Park remains open from 6 am to 11 am in the morning and 2:30 pm to 5:30 pm in the afternoon.
